LIFE GAME

is a creative simulation game of life that seeks to provide an environment for participants to discover God’s purposes for their lives in this world.

LIFE GAME is about living life and that’s where the reality of the real world sets in. Eventually, each participant will question, “What’s the point of life?” and that, is the whole point of the LIFE GAME—an opportunity to discover God’s purpose for living.
